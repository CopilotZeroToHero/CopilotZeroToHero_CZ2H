## 🏗️ Setting Up Power Platform Environments

- **Logical containers** for apps, flows, chatbots, and data.
- Plan your environment structure: Development, Test/UAT, Production.
- Use security roles, DLP policies, and monitoring for governance.
- Leverage the Center of Excellence (CoE) Starter Kit for best practices.
- **Key takeaway:** Governance is ongoing—empower makers while keeping IT oversight strong.

---

## 🏞️ Establishing an Azure AI Landing Zone

- A pre-planned cloud environment for AI workloads.
- Addresses governance, networking, identity, compliance, and resource organization.
- Reference architectures and step-by-step guidance for deploying Azure OpenAI and other AI services.
- **Benefits:** Scalable, secure, and repeatable setup for advanced AI and Copilot extensions.

---

## 🤖 Custom Copilot Extensions

- Extend Copilot with plugins, connectors, and agents.
- Connect to internal systems (ERP, CRM, etc.).
- Use Copilot Studio for low-code extensions.
- Secure APIs, register plugins, and apply governance.
- **Real-world example:** Build a “Sales Copilot” that fetches CRM data or schedules meetings via natural language.
