## 📝 Key Features & Insights

- **Copilot vs. Teams Premium**  
  | Feature                | Teams Basic | Teams Premium | Microsoft 365 Copilot |
  |------------------------|:-----------:|:-------------:|:---------------------:|
  | Intelligent Recap      | ❌          | ✅ Notes      | ✅ Custom Qs          |
  | Real-time Questions    | ❌          | ❌            | ✅                    |
  | Facilitator Agent      | ❌          | ❌            | ✅                    |
  | Interpreter Agent      | ❌          | ❌            | ✅ 9 languages        |
  | Private Copilot Chat   | ❌          | ❌            | ✅                    |

- **Operational Modes**  
  - 🎥 **Record Mode:** Full compliance (video, audio, transcript)
  - 📝 **Transcribe Mode:** Speech-to-text, no audio recording
  - 🤖 **Allow Copilot Mode:** Auto transcription, flexible storage

- **Recent Policy Changes**  
  - Separate meeting/event policies
  - Default transcription for new tenants
  - Voice/face profile enrollment for Teams Rooms
  - Organizer ownership changes (June 2024)

- **Mixed License Meetings**  
  - Organizer’s license controls access
  - Non-licensed attendees: No private Copilot/Interpreter, but can see Facilitator
  - Flexible access controls for transcripts and recaps

- **Voice & Calling**  
  - Copilot available for 1:1/group VoIP (M365 + Copilot)
  - PSTN: Needs Teams Phone + Calling Plan + Copilot
  - Teams Phone devices: AI summaries, in-call prompts

- **Compliance & Privacy**  
  - Participant notifications for recording/transcription
  - Consent management and data residency options
  - Granular access controls for meeting artifacts

---

## 🏆 Key Takeaways

- Organizer’s license and settings govern meeting experience and AI features.
- Facilitator = public AI responses; Copilot = private, personalized help.
- 'Only During' mode = privacy, no data retention; 'During & After' = full AI, data saved.
- Change management and user education are essential for success.
